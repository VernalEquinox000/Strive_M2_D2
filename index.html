Tool

Aenima

Vulcano - 1996

Tool emerged from the alternative scene in early '90s and after an ep and their debut album, after their first and to date only change in the lineup (English-born bassist Justin Chancellor entered the band after Paul D'Amour left) and went on to release "Aenima" in 1996. The album was released on lp and cd from Volcano Records, the vinyl is unavailable and a much sought gem by collectors. The smashing first track "Stinkfist", released as single with the hallucinating video directed by guitarist Adam Jones, who worked as makeup artist for Hollywood movies, is just the first punch of an album which has its best moment with "46&2", "Aenema", "Hooker With a Penis" along with crazy short tracks like "Message to Harry Manback". A true masterpiece which boosted Tool's career before their much acclaimed follow up "Lateralus".
